Background Information

As the sun and moon first formed, Pangu, the everlasting deity of the immortal plane descended, forming the first four initial elements and constructing the current cultivation rankings…

Eons have passed, life and death cycled endlessly throughout the ever-engulfing void, new individuals, new life flourished as civilizations grew to were they currently stand, a truly beautiful sight which embellished one’s sight.

On the 179th year of the Xian Calendar, our protagonist Bread was born, shaped and equipped with nine true immortal physiques and twenty-five different constitution, he conquer the now stable mortal realms and leaded the peasant towards a new era were all beings are held equal and strong, against foreign invaders.
